FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know about Upgrade 2025

Who can apply for Upgrade 2025?

Upgrade 2025 is open to anyone who is passionate about learning new skills, discovering their purpose, and becoming entrepreneurs. You don't need any prior experience or qualifications - just a willing heart and commitment to growth.

Is there a registration fee?

Yes, there is a registration fee of #5,000.

Where is the training held?

While the orientation and empowernment summit are to be held at the State Church, the training venues are dependent on the courses that the individual registered for and any location that is most suitable for the assigned facilitator(s). However Tech courses are most probable to be held virtualy.

What happens after registration?

After registration, the applicants will be added to a community on whatsapp where subsequent announcements will be coomunicated.

Can I join more than one skill category?

Students are advised to register for only one course to ensure maximization of the benefits of the course.

What is the time commitment required?

There is no standardized time commitment across courses. Lecture time will be communicated to each class by their respective facilitator(s)

Will I receive a certificate?

Every student who meets the criteria for a certificate will be issued one at the end of the programme. This criteria includes due registration and payment of fees, above 80% attendance, completion of assignments and tasks etc.

What if I can't attend every session?

Students are encouraged to attend every lecture and when not possible to duly inform their facilitator well before time. Howeever no student will be allowed to be absent to more than 3 classes in the course of the programme.

Are materials and tools provided?

The registration fee only covers for the certificate and I.D cards. Students will be responsible for any other materials required by their facilitators for the course.

What support is available after the program ends?

Upgrade 2025 graduates join the Youth Alive Fellowship alumni network, which provides: Ongoing mentorship, Business support and guidance, Networking events, Access to resources and opportunities, Community of like-minded entrepreneurs. Your growth doesn't end when the program ends!

Can I change my skill category after registration?

Skill category changes may be possible before orientation day on September 13, depending on availability. After orientation and training begins, category changes are generally not permitted to ensure quality learning outcomes. Choose carefully when registering!
