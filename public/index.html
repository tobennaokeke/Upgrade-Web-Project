ABOUT THE PROGRAM

UPGRADE is a transformative 6-week youth empowerment initiative by Youth Alive Fellowship Ikorodu, Lagos. We are on a mission to equip young people with practical skills, entrepreneurial mindset, and purpose-driven vision.

Through hands-on training, expert mentorship, and community support, participants will discover their potential and launch their journey to financial independence and personal growth.
